 Visitor Visa for Employment-Seeking Purpose 

 Requirements 
 Description 

 Completed & signed application form 
 Please access the website: https://visawebapp.boca.gov.tw/ to fill out application form online and print it out. 

 Completed & signed &ldquo;ROC EMPLOYMENT-SEEKING VISA APPLICATION CHECKLIST&rdquo; 
 Download &rdquo; ROC EMPLOYMENT-SEEKING VISA APPLICATION CHECKLIST &rdquo; 

 Two passport-size (45 mm in height and 35 mm in width) photos in color within 6 months 
 Photos with a white color background 

 Passport (original & photocopy) 
 Valid for 6 months with blank pages (excluding temporary & emergency passport) 

 Proof of meeting one of the following conditions

 Employment experience, with an average monthly salary or remuneration over the past six months that is not lower than the amount ,NT$47,971 
 Graduated within the past year from one of the world's top 1,500 universities as listed by the ROC Ministry of Education, and not yet employed 
 Deemed otherwise eligible by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs following consultations with the central competent authority 

 Proof of average monthly salary or remuneration over the past six months (only for applicants with work experience) 

 Certificate of highest education level attained and proof of employment 

 Please click the website below to check whether the university you graduated from is listed among the world's top 1,500 universities. 
 https://www.edu.tw/News_Content.aspx?n=4F8ED5441E33EA7B&s=CDFF9AA16FCD7C70# 

 Proof of sufficient financial resources ( at least NT$100,000 or its equivalent ) 

 Proof of health and full hospitalization insurance for the entire duration of stay in the ROC (Taiwan) 

 Certificate of good conduct 

 Plan for seeking employment 

 Other supporting documents (if requested) 

 Procedures： 

 Applicants can lodge their applications with the necessary documents and statutory fee at our overseas missions. 
 Interview may be required when necessary. 

 Notices： 

 Visas are issued as stipulated by Article 12 of the Statute Governing Issuance of ROC Visas for Foreign Passports. As a sovereign nation, the ROC has the right to refuse applications for visas without providing any explanation for such decisions; visa application fee is not refundable. 
 Please refer to " Standard Fees for Republic of China (Taiwan) Visas in Foreign Passports(pdf file) ."
