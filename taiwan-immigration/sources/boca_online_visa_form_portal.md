### Important Announcement: COVID-19 Medical Expenses

As per regulations of Taiwan Central Epidemic Command Center, non-R.O.C. nationals not enrolled in the NHI program should pay their medical expenses during their isolation period if they are diagnosed with COVID-19 in Taiwan. Please refer to the official press release link for more details.

### Note for ROC(Taiwan) Visa Applicants: Polio Vaccination Requirement

In accordance with World Health Organization recommendations, applicants for ROC (Taiwan) visas must provide proof of having been administered the oral polio vaccine (OPV) or the inactivated polio vaccine (IPV) from four weeks to one year before the date of their visa application submission if they have resided in or visited countries at high risk for polio transmission for four weeks or longer within the past year.

**High-risk countries for polio transmission:**
- Afghanistan
- Pakistan
- The Democratic Republic of the Congo
- Guiana
- Algeria
- Djibouti
- Israel
- Cameroon
- Chad

Please notice that the information you fill in online, including Surname, Given Name, Date of Birth, Passport No., Nationality and Sex, must completely match the information on your travel document; otherwise, your eVisa will be invalid.
