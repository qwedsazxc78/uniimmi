​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​Visa processing times guide

The visa processing times guide gives an indication of how long a visa application may take to process. This estimated timeframe is based on how long it has taken to process recently decided applications. It does not guarantee that applications will be decided within the timeframe.

the tool does not check specific information relating to your application
