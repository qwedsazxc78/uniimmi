Minors

In case of minors, application forms should be signed by the parent holding parental responsibility or legal guardian;

When minors are not travelling with both parents, or are travelling with a third person, the following documents must be presented:

a travel authorisation from the parent with whom they are not travelling or from both, with a recognised signature, duly legalised, or a court decision (where applicable) authorising the minor to travel and remain in Portugal for the period foreseen in accordance with the reason for the stay; and,

a photocopy of the parents' identity card.
