Travel Medical Insurance

Medical Travel Insurance and Health Insurance

The insurance should be valid for the entire territory of Member-States and cover the entire duration of stay or transit of the visa applicant. The insurance should be valid only for the duration of the stay and not for the duration of the visa.

The insurance should cover medical expenses including those of medical repatriation, medical emergency and/or hospital emergency, and de minimum required coverage is 30,000 Euros.

Exceptions

Are exempt from providing insurance:

Holders of diplomatic passports;

Family members of EU Member States citizens or Swiss citizens;

Airport transit visa applicants.

Exemption from insurance may be applicable to certain professional categories, such as, for example, maritime professions, already covered by the required professional insurance.
