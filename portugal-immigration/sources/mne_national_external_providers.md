External Service Providers

To ensure proximity of service to visa applicants, Portugal has delegated in certified service providers the possibility to lodge Schengen visa applications, in different cities. This allows for a more rapid response and for better territorial coverage, resulting in more applicants being served.

Portugal has signed contracts in 25 different countries, which translates into a territorial coverage of 89 cities.
