Visa fees

For each visa a fee is to be paid for the analysis of the application. The payment of the fee does not imply granting of the visa, nor does it entail refund in case of refusal.

The request is considered admissible following the payment of the required visa.

Visa Fees:

General fee is 90 Euros

Reduced fee is 45 Euros

Reduced fee

The following people benefit from a reduced fee:

Children over 6 and under 12 years of age.

Visa fee provided for EU Visa Facilitation Agreements

Countries with which the European Union has facilitation agreements:

Albania, Armenia, Azerbaijan, Bosnia, Bielorus ( partial suspension ), Georgia, Macedonia, Moldova, Montenegro, Russia (suspended - decision ), Serbia and Ukraine - 35€;

Cabo Verde

Adults - 67,75€

Children between 12-18 years - 33,75€

Children up to 12 years - exempted from paying visa fee

Visa fee applied under an implementing decision adopted by the Council to certain nationalities

Exemption

The following people are exempt from paying any fee:

Children below the age of 6 (at the date of filing of visa application);

Students and teachers/professors travelling for training or study purposes;

Researchers travelling for scientific research purposes;

Representatives of non-profit organizations under 25 years of age attending seminars, conferences, cultural/sports events, organized by non-profit organizations;

Family members of EU Member States nationals, EEA countries, Swiss nationals and family members of UK nationals under the Withdrawal Agreement.

Information on exchange rates

Fees are charged in Euros, in the currency of the third country, or in the currency commonly used in the third country where the visa application is being filed.

When filed in a currency other than Euro, the fee to be charged will be determined and revised on a regular basis. The exchange rate to be applied is that of the European Central Bank.

Information on service fees

A service fee may be charged by an external service provider. The service fee shall not exceed half of the amount of the visa fee, irrespective of the possible reductions in or exemptions from the visa fee.

However, there are derogations applicable to the mentioned limit. The service fee shall, in principle, not exceed 80 euros in third countries where Portugal has no consulate for the purpose of collecting applications and is not represented by another Member State.

In exceptional circumstances where the amount referred is not enough to provide a full service, a higher fee of up to a maximum of 120 euros may be charged.

Appeal procedure

For the administrative costs of handling each appeal procedure, for the use of administrative guarantee, to contest a visa applications decision, with the exception of applications resulting from authorizations for family reunification, 75€ ( n.º 5 of article 15º of the Order n.º 229/2021, of the 28th of October ).
