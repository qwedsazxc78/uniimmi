# DE Rantau Nomad Pass (Malaysia Digital Nomad Visa)

The DE Rantau Nomad Pass is a Professional Visit Pass (Pas Lawatan Ikhtisas) designed for foreign digital professionals who wish to live and work remotely in Malaysia. It is managed by the Malaysia Digital Economy Corporation (MDEC).

---

### Key Features

- **Duration:** 3 to 12 months.
- **Renewal:** Can be renewed for an additional 12 months (for a total of up to 24 months).
- **Dependents:** Main pass holders can bring their spouse, children, and even parents.

---

### Who Can Apply?

The pass is open to both **Digital Freelancers / Independent Contractors** and **Remote Workers**.

1.  **Digital Freelancer / Independent Contractor:**
    - Must have active project contracts of more than 3 months (can be multiple contracts).
    - Clients can be either Malaysian or foreign companies.

2.  **Remote Worker (Full-time or Part-time):**
    - Must have an active employment contract longer than 3 months.
    - Employer **must be a foreign (non-Malaysian) company**.

---

### Income & Profession Requirements

#### 1. Tech Professionals
- **Professions:** Software/Backend/Cloud Engineers, Cybersecurity, Blockchain, AI, Machine Learning, Data-related roles, Digital Marketing, Digital Content Creation, UI/UX, etc.
- **Minimum Annual Income:** **USD 24,000**

#### 2. Non-Tech Professionals
- **Professions:** Founders/C-suite (CEO, COO, CFO), Business Development, Marketing, Sales, HR, Legal, PR, Consultants, Customer Service, Technical Writers, Tax Specialists, etc.
- **Minimum Annual Income:** **USD 60,000**

---

### Fees & Other Details

- **Main Applicant Fee:** RM 1,000
- **Dependent Fee:** RM 500 per dependent.
- **Application Location:** You are not required to be in Malaysia to apply for this pass.
- **Eligible Nationalities:** Open to all nationalities except Israel.
