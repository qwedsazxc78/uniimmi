## professional visit passA Professional Visit Pass (PVP) is granted to foreign talents with acceptable professional qualifications or skills. They can enter the country and provide services or undergo practical training with a Malaysian company on behalf of an overseas company on a temporary basis, for up to 12 months.

- Before you apply
Check to see if the company is eligible to apply for a Professional Visit Pass. If eligible, company must register with the ESD portal
- Where to apply
Go to the ESD website, log in through the company’s ESD account and submit the application
- Duration of pass
No longer than 12 months per issuance
- Work rights
Expatriates may only work for the company named in the Professional Visit Pass
- Other Passes
PVP holders are not entitled to apply for a Dependant Pass
