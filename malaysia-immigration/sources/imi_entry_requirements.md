# Syarat Kemasukan Utama ke Malaysia (Main Entry Requirements for Malaysia)

This document summarizes the main requirements for foreigners entering Malaysia. The original source page is a portal with embedded content, so this file serves as a more readable summary with direct links.

---

### 1. Pasport atau Dokumen Perjalanan yang Sah (Valid Passport or Travel Document)

- All foreign nationals must possess a valid national passport or other internationally recognized travel document.
- The passport must be valid for **at least six (6) months** from the date of entry into Malaysia.

---

### 2. Visa

- Foreign nationals must obtain a visa unless they are from a visa-exempt country.
- For detailed visa requirements by country, refer to the local source file:
  - `[Keperluan Visa Mengikut Negara (Visa Requirements by Country)](./imi_visa_by_country.md)`
- Or check the official IMI website.

---

### 3. Malaysia Digital Arrival Card (MDAC)

- **Effective 1 January 2024**, all foreign nationals are required to complete and submit the MDAC online.
- This must be done within **three (3) days prior** to arrival in Malaysia.
- **Official MDAC Registration Website:** <https://imigresen-online.imi.gov.my/mdac/main>
- Exemptions apply to:
  - Singapore passport holders
  - Diplomatic and Official passport holders
  - Malaysian Permanent Residents and Long-Term Pass holders

---

### 4. Keperluan Berkaitan COVID-19 (COVID-19 Related Requirements)

- As of 1 August 2022, all travelers are allowed to enter Malaysia regardless of their COVID-19 vaccination status.
- Pre-departure and on-arrival COVID-19 tests are no longer required.
- No quarantine orders related to COVID-19 are currently in effect.
