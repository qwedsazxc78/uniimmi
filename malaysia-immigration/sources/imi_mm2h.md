GENERAL NOTES:
- MM2H participant are allowed to withdraw partial of the required Fixed Deposit startingfrom the second year onwards(effective from the date of MM2H Social Visit Pass endorsement in the passport) for approved expenses related to residential purchase, children’s education expenses, medical expenses as per below:
(a) RM50,000 for aged 50 years and above; and
(b) RM150,000 for aged below 50 years.
However, MM2H participant are required to maintain the balanced of Fixed Deposit until they terminate their participation from Programme as per below:
(a) RM 100,000 for aged 50 years and above; and
(b) RM 150,000 for aged below 50 years.
- Withdrawal of Fixed Deposit is on reimbursement basis and release of money will be made based on the amount requested.
- All application for withdrawal of fixed deposit is no longer permitted to be submitted by e-mail.
- Any incomplete documents will not be processed.
- Complete documents will be process within three (3) working days.
