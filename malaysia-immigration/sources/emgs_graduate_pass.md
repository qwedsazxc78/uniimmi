### What is the Graduate Pass?
Malaysia offers a Graduate Pass for international students. It is a Social Visit Pass that enables international students to stay in Malaysia for a year after graduation. It lets you travel and work in permitted job sectors.
Graduate Pass is a policy introduced by the Ministry of Home Affairs and issued by the Department of Immigration Malaysia.

### EVERYTHING YOU SHOULD KNOW ABOUT THE GRADUATE PASS BEFORE YOU APPLYEligibility Requirements for International Students
- Your passport must be valid for at least 18 months.
- The Graduate Pass is only issued to students who have completed a Bachelor’s degree or higher.
- You must provide a letter of completion from your educational institution proving you have successfully completed your course.
- You must hold a valid Student Pass during the Graduate Pass application process.
- You must have an active health insurance policy for the duration of the Graduate Pass.
- You must provide a personal bond with a Malaysian citizen as your sponsor, who must have a minimum monthly salary of RM 1,500.
- The student must not have any legal convictions in Malaysia.Eligible Nationalities
